Stage 1 — Vendor Pre-Qualification
Simple definition
A short, guided onboarding where a vendor provides required legal/compliance info (private) and public profile info. When submitted, the account moves to Under Review until Bid Ops verifies it.
Plain goal (one sentence)
Collect just enough vendor info to verify identity and create a simple public profile—fast, clear, and reviewable.
Ready-to-Paste Guideline (for Replit AI Agent)
Role: You are a senior product manager and UX designer implementing Stage 1: Vendor Pre-Qualification for the Bid platform.
Format: Return your output as a clear, structured implementation spec (no code in the response). Include: scope, flows, fields, rules, validations, microcopy, notifications, analytics events, acceptance criteria, and edge cases.
1) Scope (In / Out)
In scope
Vendor onboarding form with two sections:
Legal & Compliance (Private, not shown on profile)
Company name (as per CR), CR number, VAT cert (if applicable), GOSI cert, National Address.
Public Profile (Shown on profile)
Display name (public), Logo, Header (image/color), Short bio (100–300 words), Category/Tags (3–7), Profile File (PDF brochure, optional), Social links (LinkedIn/X/Website).
Status machine: Not Verified → Under Review → Verified (with rejection back to Not Verified).
Auto-create Vendor Profile when vendor submits the pre-qualification.
Conditional submission rule (tender-level): allow or block submission while vendor is Under Review.
Out of scope (MVP)
Stats/ratings/portfolio grids on profile.
Any public display of legal documents or numbers.
Action buttons (Invite to Tender / Join Vendors Base).
Payments, messaging, or advanced verification automations.
2) User Flows (High level)
2.1 Vendor — Pre-Qualification
Step 1: Sign in → Pre-Qualification starts.
Step 2: Fill Legal & Compliance (private).
Step 3: Fill Public Profile (name/logo/header/bio/category/profile PDF/socials).
Step 4: Inline validation → Submit for Review.
Step 5: Status becomes Under Review; Vendor Profile is auto-created (public).
Step 6 (later): Bid Ops verifies → Verified OR rejects → Not Verified (+reason).
2.2 Requester — During Proposal Review (context)
While viewing a proposal, can open vendor’s public profile (name/logo/header/bio/category/profile PDF/socials) to understand who they are.
3) Status Model & Rules
Vendor Account Status
NOT_VERIFIED: default; can’t submit (unless tender allows conditional submission? No—must be at least Under Review).
UNDER_REVIEW: after vendor submits pre-qualification; may submit only if the tender has allow_conditional_submission = true. Awards/evaluation can be held until verification.
VERIFIED: full submission and award eligibility.
Transitions
NOT_VERIFIED → UNDER_REVIEW (on submit).
UNDER_REVIEW → VERIFIED (approved by Ops).
UNDER_REVIEW → NOT_VERIFIED (rejected, with reason).
Tender policy toggle
allow_conditional_submission (default: false).
If true: Under Review vendors may submit; award is blocked until verified.
If false: vendors must be Verified to submit.
4) Fields & Validations
4.1 Legal & Compliance (Private)
Company name (as per CR) — required, text; 2–120 chars.
CR number — required, text; normalize to digits; length check.
VAT certificate — optional if not registered; upload file (PDF/JPG/PNG), max 10MB; capture VAT number if present.
GOSI certificate — required if applicable; upload file (PDF/JPG/PNG), max 10MB.
National Address — required; structured (address1, city, region, postal code, country).
Note: All legal documents remain private (never shown on public profile).
4.2 Public Profile (Shown)
Display name (public) — required; 2–60 chars.
Logo — optional; image; recommended 512×512 min.
Header — optional; image or color token.
Short bio — required; 100–300 words; plain text.
Category/Tags — required; 3–7 items (free text or controlled list).
Profile File (PDF brochure) — optional; max 10MB; 1–5 pages recommended.
Social links — optional; LinkedIn, X, Website (validate URL).
General validation
Required fields must be completed to enable Submit for Review.
Show a completeness progress bar with checklist of what’s missing.
Accept files: PDF/JPG/PNG; max 10MB; basic virus/mime check.
5) UX & Microcopy (short, human)
Buttons
“Save as draft”
“Submit for review”
Inline help
“Legal documents are private and used only for verification.”
“Your profile helps requesters recognize your brand.”
“Keep bio short, clear, and focused on your services.”
“Add 3–7 categories that best describe your work.”
“Optional: upload a one-pager PDF to introduce your company.”
Errors
“Please enter your CR number.”
“Logo must be an image under 10MB.”
“Bio is too short—aim for at least 100 words.”
Success
“Submitted. Your account is now Under Review.”

6) Notifications (MVP)
Vendor (on submit): “Thanks! Your account is under review.”
Vendor (approved): “You’re verified. Your submissions are fully eligible.”
Vendor (rejected): “We couldn’t verify your account: {reason}. Please update and resubmit.”
7) Privacy & Visibility
Public profile shows only: name, logo, header, bio, category/tags, status (text badge), profile PDF link, and social links.
Legal/compliance fields are strictly private (Ops use only).
Status is visible as text: Not Verified / Under Review / Verified—no numbers, no certificate images.

vendor_prequal_submitted {vendor_id, has_profile_pdf, category_count}
vendor_status_changed {vendor_id, from, to, by_role}
profile_created_auto {vendor_id}
profile_viewed {viewer_role} (e.g., Requester viewing during evaluation)
submission_blocked_due_to_status {vendor_id, tender_id, required_status}
conditional_submission_allowed {tender_id}
9) Acceptance Criteria (Definition of Done)
Form & Flow
Vendor can complete legal & profile sections with inline validation and progress bar.
Submit is disabled until required fields are complete.
On submit, status changes to Under Review and a public profile is auto-created.
Visibility & Privacy
Public profile shows only approved public fields; no legal data.
Status badge visible on the public profile (text only).
Rules
If tender has allow_conditional_submission = false, Under Review vendors cannot submit (clear message).
If true, Under Review vendors can submit, but award is blocked until Verified.
Comms & Analytics
Confirmation messages and three notification templates exist.
Listed analytics events are emitted with stated properties.
10) Edge Cases
No logo/header → show neutral placeholders.
No profile PDF → hide the field entirely (no dead link).
Broken social URLs → suppress the link on public view; prompt vendor in edit view only.
Rejection → revert to Not Verified with reason; vendor can edit and resubmit.
Name mismatch → “Display name” is public; “Legal name (CR)” remains private and separate.
